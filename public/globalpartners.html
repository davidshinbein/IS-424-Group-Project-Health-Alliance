Our Global Partners

For our Global Partner, we partner with the Village Health Project (VHP), which is both a nonprofit and student organization on campus. VHP operates in the community of Lweza, Uganda. In this community, they aim to build sustainable projects that are related to necessities such as strong food and sanitation systems.

At the end of each semester, a large portion of our fundraising efforts goes towards VHP to help the progression of their projects. To learn more about them, visit their website at https://www.villagehealthproject.org/ .
