Welcome to UW-Madison's Global Health Alliance!

About Us

About us:
We are a group of passionate, intellectually diverse students who come together to learn about global health and engage in local and international partnerships to promote health equity and social justice. We focus on improving health equity through student led initiatives of service, fundraising, and education. Our partnerships are both local and international, and focused on being as equitable, sustainable, and involved as possible.

Forum:
Our weekly forums consist of getting together to learn and discuss different Global Health topics. Past examples include health policy, food systems, environmental health, and healthy aging. We also often have guest speakers come in to discuss various topics pertaining to their work and experiences.

Local Partnership:
Our local partnership events consist of volunteering in the UW-Madison and Greater Madison Area. Past groups that we have partnered with include Food Recovery Network, Lakeshore Nature Preserve, and Porchlight.

Campaigns (Fundraising):
Our campaigns consist of having internal and external fundraisers. Past examples of internal examples include bake sales, tote bag painting, and candy bag sales. For external events, we’ve partnered with Nitty Gritt, Ian’s, and Taggs.

Community Building (Social):
Our community building events consist of socials, where we meet to build connections within the club. Past events include painting socials, GHA-giving, and campfires at Picnic Point.

This alliance spans across multiple college campuses, including Amherst University, MIT, Tufts University, University of Chicago, University of Michigan, University of Texas-Austin, and UW-Madison. We envision a world of peace and prosperity that promotes equitable health for all people.
